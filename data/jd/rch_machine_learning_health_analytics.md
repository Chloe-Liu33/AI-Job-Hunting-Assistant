## About the hospital

The Royal Children's Hospital has cared for the children and young people of
Victoria for more than 150 years (founded 1870). It provides a full range of
paediatric and adolescent health services plus tertiary and quaternary care for
the most critically ill and medically complex patients in Victoria, Tasmania,
southern NSW and beyond. RCH has 6,000+ staff, an $850M budget, 12 wards and
350 beds, with 300,000+ specialist clinic appointments, 90,000+ ED
presentations and 20,000 elective surgeries annually. RCH is a cornerstone
member of the Melbourne Children's Campus alongside Murdoch Children's Research
Institute, the University of Melbourne Department of Paediatrics and the RCH
Foundation.

## About the department

The Centre for Health Analytics enables Melbourne Children's to become an
internationally leading paediatric campus in the use of data to improve patient
care, operations, education and research. The Centre delivers data services
across the Campus, builds data infrastructure, and develops a data-enabled
workforce — its vision being to unleash the power of data to improve health.

## About the role

You will deliver advanced analytic projects; provide expert guidance and
advice; manage capability development and supervision; and ensure successful
delivery of those projects. The role contributes to long-term strategic
outcomes including the development of **reusable AI products** leveraging
hospital data assets and CHA's sociotechnical and process stack.
