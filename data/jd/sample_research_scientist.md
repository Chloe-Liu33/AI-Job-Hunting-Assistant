# Research Scientist — Spatio-Temporal Machine Learning

## About the role
We are hiring a Research Scientist to develop next-generation forecasting models for city-scale systems. You will design and ship deep learning models for spatio-temporal prediction, working with large mobility and sensor datasets.

## Responsibilities
- Build and evaluate models for traffic / demand forecasting at city scale.
- Develop interpretable and trustworthy ML methods suitable for high-stakes deployment.
- Publish at top venues (NeurIPS, KDD, ICLR) and collaborate with engineering to productionize.
- Work with multimodal data (sensor, graph, temporal) and attention-based fusion.

## Requirements
- PhD (or near completion) in CS, ML, or related field.
- Strong track record in spatio-temporal modeling, graph neural networks, or time-series forecasting.
- Proficiency in Python and PyTorch.
- Publications at peer-reviewed ML/data-mining venues.
- Experience with interpretability / trustworthy AI is a strong plus.

## Nice to have
- Experience with healthcare or clinical prediction tasks.
- Familiarity with multilingual / cross-domain transfer.
