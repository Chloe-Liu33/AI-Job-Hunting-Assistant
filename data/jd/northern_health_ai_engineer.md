## About Northern Health

Northern Health is the major provider of acute, maternity, sub-acute, mental
health, specialist, community and home-based services in Melbourne's rapidly
growing outer north. Services span six main campuses — Northern Hospital
Epping, Broadmeadows Hospital, Bundoora Centre, Craigieburn and Mernda
Community Hospitals, and Kilmore District Hospital — plus mental health
services across Epping, Broadmeadows, Jacana, Preston, Mill Park, Mernda and
Coburg.

## About the role

The AI Engineer delivers modern artificial intelligence capabilities that
improve speed, efficiency, quality and decision-making across Northern Health.

The role provides specialist expertise in data science, large language models
(LLMs), agentic AI systems, machine learning, analytics and software
engineering to design, build and deploy scalable digital health solutions.

It supports the Associate Director, Digital Innovation and Research to rapidly
identify opportunities, prototype solutions and operationalise AI products that
create measurable clinical, operational and financial value.

The role will help transition the organisation from traditional predictive
machine learning toward contemporary generative AI, intelligent automation,
**retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), AI copilots, and multi-agent
workflows**.

## Key responsibilities

- Design, build and deploy scalable digital health AI solutions.
- Prototype rapidly and operationalise AI products with measurable clinical,
  operational and financial value.
- Apply LLMs, generative AI, RAG, AI copilots and multi-agent workflows.
- Advance the shift from traditional predictive ML to contemporary generative
  AI and intelligent automation.

## Requirements

- Degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, Software Engineering,
  AI, or a related field.
- Proven experience in AI, machine learning, and data analytics.
- Proficiency in Python and/or R; experience with SQL and relational databases.
- Experience with cloud platforms, APIs, and modern data technologies.
- Skilled in developing, testing, and deploying AI, analytics, or digital
  solutions.
- Knowledge of ML, statistical modeling, data visualization, LLMs, generative
  AI, and prompt engineering.
- Understanding of software development practices (source control, testing,
  documentation).
- Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills; able to explain
  technical concepts to non-technical audiences.
- Ability to collaborate with technical and business stakeholders.
